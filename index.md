# ReShuffle Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

ReShuffle ("the app") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

## Data Collection

ReShuffle collects **no data**. The app does not:

- Collect personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or crash reporting services
- Share any information with third parties

## Apple Music Library Access

ReShuffle requests access to your Apple Music library solely to read your playlists and create new shuffled playlists on your device. This access:

- Happens entirely on your device
- Is never transmitted anywhere
- Is used only to perform the shuffle functionality you request

## Third-Party Services

ReShuffle uses Apple's MusicKit framework to interact with your Apple Music library. This is governed by Apple's own privacy policy at [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Children's Privacy

ReShuffle does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
